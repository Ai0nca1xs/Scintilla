This educational building-block toy is designed to help autistic children
develop their autonomy, cognitive skills and social interactions through
colour. It consists of a wooden base, modular blocks with different paths, and
figurines representing elements of everyday life. The child can create their
own maze and explore scenarios adapted to their learning.

Informed by a study of chromatic preferences, the product comes in four colour
palettes to stimulate perception and ease immersion in interactive play
scenarios. Three difficulty levels — beginner, intermediate, advanced —
accompany the child's progression.

The project is part of the "Nouveaux Pousseurs" programme, developed in
collaboration with special-education institutions and grounded in in-depth
research on the impact of colour on children's cognitive abilities.
