Born from wandering through the Base sous-marine in Bordeaux, this editorial
project rereads the monument as a palimpsest: a surface written, erased and
rewritten — where concrete preserves the strata of time like so many layers of
memory.

The 48-page zine gathers personal research conducted on the site, extended
through photographic series and original texts. The layout works with
superimposition, erasure and reappearance as graphic principles, echoing the
very nature of the place.
