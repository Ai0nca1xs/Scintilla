The analysis explores the relationship between the colours of new collections
from major makeup brands and the annual shades defined by bodies such as
Pantone. In a context of economic crisis and shifting consumer behaviour,
interest is moving towards mid-range, independent and sustainable products,
opening new opportunities for national brands.

The 2021 trend-colour forecast for Marie Dalgar unfolds into four themes —
Unrestrained, Silence, Éclats, Moderne — translated into trend collages and
chromatic posters.

For the launch event, a conceptual "Escargot" space was designed around the
idea of makeup as a semi-transparent exoskeleton wrapping a tender core.
Divided into inner and outer zones, the space offers an immersive experience
of the emerging shades: lighting tints semi-transparent materials to reinforce
the chromatic immersion of each of the four atmospheres.
